A Beauty Brandnaming & the strategy beneath it

Eight Names Two Worlds One Decision

These aren't one shortlist. They're two brands wearing one folder.

The Mini Brand Strategy
The Opportunity

Beauty is a loud, crowded category — a wall of shades, hype and influencer noise. A new brand wins not on more product, but on a sharper point of view. Two spaces remain genuinely winnable: intelligent, light-led colour that flatters every skin honestly, and makeup as effortless self-expression rather than performance. Your names split along exactly that line — which is the most useful thing a naming round can reveal.

The Audience

A modern beauty buyer — design-literate, value-driven, sceptical of hype. She wants fewer, better products that feel like her, real inclusivity, and a brand with taste and a clear idea. She buys into a point of view, not a promo.

The Strategic Read

Eight names, but two brands. Three build a world of light and clarity; five build a world of intimacy and confidence. The question isn't "which name is prettiest" — it's "which world are we building?" Choose that, and the name chooses itself.

World A

The Optics of Beauty

PRYSM · LUCID · UNDER TONE

Idea
Colour, engineered around light.
Positioning
Radiant, true-to-skin colour made with the precision of optics — beauty that reveals rather than masks.
Personality
Refined, modern, quietly expert. Scientific, never clinical.
Promise
"Your truest colour, revealed."
Best for
a premium, inclusive, clean-leaning brand built for authority and longevity.
World B

Beauty as a Gesture

W!NK · FT. YOU · FEATURES · FLIRT · WHISPERS

Idea
The small gesture that makes a big presence.
Positioning
Makeup as effortless confidence and allure — a flick, a flush, a wink — not a mask, not a routine.
Personality
Playful, warm, flirtatious, you-first. Talks like a confident friend.
Promise
"Small gesture. Big presence."
Best for
a younger, expressive, social-first brand with attitude and built-in shareability.
Tone of Voice

World A speaks precise and luminous — fewer words, more light. World B speaks cheeky and intimate — a wink in words.

World A

The Optics of Beauty

01

PRYSM

Split the light. Own the spectrum.

A prism takes one beam of white light and reveals the entire spectrum hiding inside it — the perfect metaphor for a brand that holds every shade. The deliberate PRYSM spelling makes it modern, designed and ownable where "prism" never could be. It turns colour into a science you want to wear — and the spectrum metaphor builds inclusivity in from day one.

  • The verbal range
  • Split the light. Own the spectrum.
  • Colour, refracted. Beauty, refined.
  • Turn angle into aura.
  • One shape. Infinite shades.
The hook · every shade, from one beam
02

LUCID

Purity of light. Depth of shade.

Lucid means clear, luminous and vivid — with a dreamlike second life in "lucid dream." For beauty it promises both glow and honesty: luminous skin, true colour, clean transparency. Calm, premium and a touch poetic — a real word elevated into an ownable idea.

  • The verbal range
  • See your glow, clearly.
  • Purity of light. Depth of shade.
  • Awaken the vivid.
  • Dreams in colour, clarity in wear.
The hook · clarity you can wear
03

UNDER TONE

Light your look.

Undertone is the word every shade-match begins with — the hue beneath the skin that decides what flatters you. Owning it signals real expertise in matching colour to every complexion, and quietly promises inclusivity. The split — UNDER · TONE — adds a second beat: understated, but precisely yours.

  • The verbal range
  • Light your look.
  • Wear sunlight — minus the wait.
  • Soft radiance, strong impact.
The hook · where every shade-match starts
World B

Beauty as a Gesture

04

W!NK

Let your lids do the flirting.

A wink is flirtation distilled into a single gesture — and "in a blink" makes it effortless. Swapping the i for an exclamation mark turns the name into a ready-made logo and literalises the whole promise: small gesture, big !. Young, confident and instantly memorable.

  • The verbal range
  • Small gesture. Big impact.
  • Get noticed in a blink.
  • Tiny gesture, major impact.
  • Let your lids do the flirting.
The hook · the flirt, in one keystroke
05

ft. You

The spotlight belongs to you.

The sharpest, most original idea in the set. "ft." is the music-credit shorthand for featuring — so the product bills you as the star and casts itself as the supporting act. It flips beauty's whole logic from mask to amplifier, nails the on-trend "you-first" truth, and speaks fluent culture to a younger audience. A name you could build a universe around. (Set lowercase, the way a track credit always is.)

  • The verbal range
  • Starring pigment, subtle touch.
  • Quick swipe, headline look.
  • Minimal effort, maximum presence.
  • The spotlight belongs to you.
The hook · you headline; makeup just features
06

FEATURES

Highlight what's already yours.

Features is two ideas in one elegant word. First, your features — the eyes, lips and cheekbones makeup exists to flatter — which casts the brand as an enhancer, never a mask. Second, featuring — the film-and-music sense of top billing — so the line quietly makes you the star. It's the cool, grown-up cousin of ft. You: the same you-first truth, told as a confident noun instead of a playful credit. Minimal, modern and effortlessly premium — a single word that doubles as a promise.

  • The verbal range
  • We don't cover. We feature.
  • Your features, in focus.
  • Starring: you.
  • Less makeup. More you.
The hook · your features, top billing
07

FLIRT

Soft glide, loud heartbeat.

One bold, emotional word for the oldest truth in beauty — the spark, the magnetism, the play. Direct, cheeky and confident, it hands a brand instant personality. A note for the brief: "Flirt" has appeared in beauty before, so it'll need a hard trademark check — but as an idea, it's pure attitude.

  • The verbal range
  • Whisper-light colour, undeniable spark.
  • Bare touch, vivid attraction.
  • Soft glide, loud heartbeat.
The hook · attraction, distilled to a word
08

WHISPERS

Barely a whisper, plenty to say.

A whisper is intimate, soft and quietly powerful — the perfect name for the "quiet luxury" and no-makeup-makeup moment. It promises subtle allure with a lingering afterglow: restraint that still turns heads. Elegant and sensual — and the plural makes it feel like a collection of secrets.

  • The verbal range
  • Soft hush, striking afterglow.
  • Gentle sweep, lingering allure.
  • Barely a whisper, plenty to say.
The hook · the quiet that says everything
Where to From Here

Resolve the fork first — World A or World B. It sets everything downstream: the identity, the audience, even how you name the products.

  • The most ownable, build-a-universe names are PRYSM — distinctive spelling, rich metaphor — for World A, and ft. You — genuinely original, culturally perfect — for World B.
  • Features and ft. You are one idea in two registers — the cool, editorial noun and the playful music-credit. Pick the voice: Features reads grown-up and confident; ft. You reads young and witty. Run one, not both.
  • UNDER TONE is the most strategically credible if inclusivity and shade expertise lead the positioning.
  • Pressure-test the common-word names — LUCID, FLIRT, WHISPERS — hardest for trademark and domain; they're beautiful but well-trodden in beauty.
  • Then shortlist two or three within the chosen world, clear trademark, social handles and the .com, and live with each as a logo, a compact and an Instagram grid before you commit.
