Don't borrow energy from elsewhere. Take it from the city.

The City Is the Brief

The task: name a new open-air retail destination in the heart of Alexandria, a place to gather, shop, eat and linger. The strategy is single-minded. Alexandria is the muse. Its founding date, its lighthouse, its sea, its rhythm, its hidden lanes, each one a different way for the destination to wear the city's name. What follows are eleven routes, sorted into four territories.

Territory I

Heritage & Story

Names that reach into 2,300 years of Alexandria and carry the city's myth into a modern address.

01

Three Thirty

Alexandria, est. 330 BCE.

A number that's secretly a birth year. Founded in 330 BCE, Alexandria carries two and a half thousand years of history, and Three Thirty wears all of it lightly. It turns a date into a destination: contemporary enough for a new retail address, deep enough to mean something no rival can claim.

The hook · a date becomes a name
02

Old Town

Where history keeps shop.

Old Town trades on instant warmth. It promises lived-in character and cobbled charm, a place that feels like it's always been here, even on opening day. It's globally understood shorthand for the soulful heart of a city, and it lends a brand-new development a sense of belonging from the very first visit.

03

BayLight

A beacon on the bay.

BayLight reaches for Alexandria's greatest icon, the ancient Lighthouse, one of the Seven Wonders, which guided ships home for centuries. It casts the destination as a modern beacon: a guiding point of gathering, discovery and coastal life. Luminous and landmark-worthy. A name that asks to be lit at night.

The hook · the Lighthouse of Alexandria
Territory II

Sea & Coast

Names drawn from the Mediterranean itself: the blue, the breeze, and the port that made the city's name.

04

Blue Sands

Turquoise water, golden shore.

Blue Sands paints the picture before you arrive: the Mediterranean's blue meeting Alexandria's warm sand. Serene, scenic and unmistakably coastal, it frames the destination as a breezy escape in the middle of the city. Soft, premium, and easy to fall for.

05

Port31

Mediterranean gateway · 31°N.

Port31 fuses two truths into one confident mark: Alexandria as a great Mediterranean port, and its place on the map at 31° north. "Port" signals trade, arrival and openness to the world; "31" makes it precise, modern and ownable. A name where commerce and coordinates meet.

The hook · latitude 31°N
06

UpNorth

The north coast, elevated.

Alexandria sits at the top of the map, and UpNorth turns geography into attitude. It carries a cool, aspirational lift, "going up north" as a little escape, pairing the Mediterranean breeze with modern polish. Confident, directional, effortlessly lifestyle.

Territory III

Rhythm & Motion

Names that move: built on the pulse and constant energy of the street.

07

AlexMotion

The city, in motion.

Alexandria never stands still: the surge of the markets, the flow along the Corniche, the breeze off the sea. AlexMotion bottles that restless energy and gives the destination a sense of perpetual life, a place that moves with the city, and keeps it moving.

08

Ten Beats

The rhythm of the city.

Ten Beats hears the centre as music, a row of businesses playing in time, the pulse of a place that's always alive. "Ten" gives it a crisp, countable identity, "beats" keeps it young and energetic. A name with a tempo.

Territory IV

Everyday & Human

Names that feel like people and habits: the place you return to without thinking.

09

Goto

Your go-to. For everything.

The sharpest piece of plain-language branding in the set. Goto is the place you name without thinking: the errand, the coffee, the meet-up, the "let's just go to…". It builds habit into the brand and quietly dreams of becoming a verb. Effortless to say, impossible to forget.

The hook · a name that becomes a verb
10

Alleyway

Wander in.

Alleyway reframes a shopping centre as a network of intimate lanes to explore rather than a corridor to march down. Narrow, characterful, full of small surprises. It borrows the charm of Alexandria's hidden backstreets and turns "getting a little lost" into the whole appeal.

11

Alex & Ria

Alexandria, on first-name terms.

The wittiest name in the room. Split "Alexandria" and you get two characters, Alex and Ria, and suddenly a place feels like people. Warm, playful and instantly memorable, it frames the destination as a friendly meeting of personalities, with a quiet nod to the partnership behind it. A human spin on a monumental name.

The hook · Alexandria → Alex & Ria
Where to From Here

A shortlist is a conversation, not a verdict. The strongest routes turn a fact about Alexandria into a feeling: a founding date, a latitude, a lighthouse, a split of the city's own name.

Because a story no competitor can copy is the most defensible asset a name can own. Recommended next step: shortlist three, pressure-test each for trademark and domain availability, and live with them on a sign, a tote bag and a storefront before deciding.
